**Headline:** Stop loss, simplified

**Step 1: Specific Stop Loss**
Protects against individual high-cost claims. You choose a deductible (e.g., $150K). When any one person's claims exceed that amount, we reimburse the excess. You're never exposed to a single catastrophic claim.

**Step 2: Aggregate Stop Loss**
Protects against your total plan costs exceeding expectations. If overall claims spike above the corridor (typically 125% of expected), we cover the difference. Your budget has a ceiling.

**Step 3: You Stay in Control**
